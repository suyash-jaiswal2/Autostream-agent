# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform providing automated AI-powered video editing tools for content creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and more.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Videos: 10 videos/month
- Resolution: 720p
- Support: Standard support

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Videos: Unlimited videos
- Resolution: 4K
- Features: AI captions included
- Support: 24/7 priority support

## Company Policies
- Refund Policy: No refunds after 7 days of purchase
- Customer Support: 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan
- Free Trial: 7-day free trial available for new users
